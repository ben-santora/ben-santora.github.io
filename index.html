Linux / CPU-Only Inference / SLM Testing

I’m an Engineering Technician currently focused on testing Small Language Models (SLMs) on the Linux Operating System working within efficient, CPU-only environments.

Work

I evaluate and integrate small language models in constrained, CPU-only environments, with an emphasis on predictability, performance, and clear system boundaries. Your work sits at the intersection of AI and systems engineering, where language models are treated as tools for reasoning, explanation, and design support rather than autonomous actors. You focus on practical workflows that combine AI-assisted thinking with established Unix tooling, preserving explicit data flow, deterministic execution, and debuggability. Current work includes assessing the real-world viability of small models such as gemma-3-4b outside GPU-centric assumptions, and designing workflows that use each layer of the system—model, shell, and operating system—according to its strengths.
