I’m an Engineering Technician with over 15 years of Linux experience, currently focused on testing Small Language Models (SLMs) in efficient, CPU-only environments.

Philosophy

My work centers on a pragmatic intersection of AI and systems engineering. I believe that while Language Models excel at reasoning, abstraction, and teaching, they shouldn't replace the deterministic power of the operating system.

"The shell is the only agent you need."

I advocate for a workflow where AI acts as an assistant—translating intent and explaining concepts—while the Linux shell handles the execution. By letting tools like Bash handle files, processes, and streams, we keep workflows fast, explicit, and resource-efficient.

Focus Areas

  • SLM Testing
    Running models like gemma-3-4b in constrained, CPU-only environments to test real-world viability.
  • Systems Engineering
    Leveraging standard Linux tools (grep, awk, pipes) for deterministic execution and performance.
  • Workflow Optimization
    Bridging the gap between AI reasoning and system-level execution.
